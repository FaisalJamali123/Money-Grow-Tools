Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Money Grow Tools collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our tools, subscribing to newsletters, or contacting us. This may include:

  • Name and email address
  • Financial information entered into our calculators (e.g., income, state)
  • Usage data including pages visited and interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our financial tools and services
  • Send newsletters, updates, or promotional emails (if subscribed)
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage trends and improve website performance

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering services or analyzing website usage. These providers are obligated to protect your data.

5. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when visiting external sites.

7. Children's Privacy

Money Grow Tools is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have inadvertently collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@moneygrowtools.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the effective date. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at: hello@moneygrowtools.com.
